Foundation & History

At Primarch Pet Cuts, our journey began with a passion for delivering exceptional pet care. We believe every pet deserves the highest grooming, comfort, and love. Our collective family is dedicated to ensuring your furry battle buddy receives the attention and care they need and deserve.

Prices & Packages

Essential Grooming

Our Essential Grooming package offers a comprehensive grooming service designed to keep your pet looking fresh and feeling clean. This package includes:

  • Bathing and drying
  • Brushing and detangling
  • Nail trimming
  • Ear cleaning
  • And more!

Advanced Grooming

For pets requiring a higher level of care, our Golden Halo Grooming package features tailored services, including:

  • De-Fur Shed treatments
  • Specialized Legion Coat Treatments
  • Luxury Spa Chapter Options

Primarch Cuts

Our Specialty Cuts are designed for special breeds and styles. This package includes artistic grooming options such as:

  • Breed-specific cuts and styles
  • Creative grooming with color and designs
  • Custom consultations to match your pet's personality

Add-on Services

Enhance your pet's experience with our add-on services, which include:

  • Teeth brushing
  • Flea and tick treatments
  • Specialized skin care

Meet Your Companion's Stylist

Our team of highly skilled stylists are not only trained in the art of grooming but are also passionate about animal care. They take the time to get to know your pet, ensuring a gentle and personalized approach to each grooming session. Every stylist at Primarch Pet Cuts is committed to continuing education to stay up-to-date with grooming techniques and trends.

Meet the Founder

Chapter Master Joshua Hill is the visionary behind Primarch Pet Cuts. With years of experience in pet care and grooming, Joshua has devoted his life to elevating pet grooming standards. He is committed to creating a safe, nurturing, and playfull environment for pets and their owners. Under his leadership, Primarch Pet Cuts will continue to innovate and excel in pet grooming services.
